Pathanamthitta Jilla

Pathanamthitta District, the thirteenth revenue district of the State of Kerala, nestles its head on the slopes of the Western Ghats and stretches to the low-lying rice fields bordering Alappuzha District. It was formed with effect from the 1st November, 1982 vide G.O. (M.S) No.1026/82/RD dated 29.10.1982, with headquarters at Pathanamthitta.

sweet spots



Gavi is a pristine and picturesque forest haven in Pathanamthitta. The place started being hailed as a “must-visit” on the release of the movie “Ordinary”. And when Alister International listed it among major eco-tourism centres and one of the must-visit places in India, it became famous amongst tourists, especially the Europeans.


Aranmula is a temple town in Kerala situated on the banks of river Pamba at a distance of 14.8 km from Pathanamthitta. It gets its name from the centuries-old Aranmula Parthasarathy Temple. Aranmula is renowned for its annual boat race, the “Aranmula Vallamkali” and Aranmula Kannadi a handmade metal-alloy mirror. The UN has enlisted this village a global heritage site.
